# VTOBForge Engineering Playbook

**Version:** 0.1.0

**Status:** In Active Development

---

## Purpose

The VTOBForge Engineering Playbook documents the engineering knowledge, standards, decisions, and practices used across every VTOBForge software project.

Its purpose is to ensure that engineering knowledge becomes institutional knowledge rather than remaining dependent on individual memory.

This playbook evolves continuously as VTOBForge builds products, solves engineering problems, and develops better practices.

---

# Engineering Philosophy

Every engineering decision should answer:

* What problem does this solve?
* Why was this approach chosen?
* What alternatives were considered?
* What trade-offs exist?
* Can future builders understand this decision?

---

# Core Principles

* Understand before modifying.
* Simplicity over unnecessary complexity.
* Document important decisions.
* Build reusable knowledge.
* Build systems that outlive individuals.
* Learn by Building.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Engineering Playbook is intended to become the primary engineering reference for every VTOBForge project.

As new products are developed, new engineering knowledge will be documented, reviewed, refined, and preserved for future builders.

The goal is not simply to build software.

The goal is to build builders who can consistently create excellent software.
